  • Token name: ChiliZ.
  • Ticker: CHZ.
  • Blockchain: Ethereum , Binance Chain.
  • Token Standard: ERC-20 , BEP-2.
  • Contract:
    • ERC-20: 0x3506424f91fd33084466f402d5d97f05f8e3b4af
    • BEP-2: bnb1cghr4z8ag440tv4wnk3l6wzynytlpvfqltm9ph
  • Decimal: 18.
  • Token type: Utility Token.
  • Consensus: Proof of Authority (PoA).
